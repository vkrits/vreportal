 Who we are
VRE4EIC project is a European project aiming in addressing key data and software challenges in supporting multidisciplinary data driven sciences. These include:
  • Understanding complex user requirements across domains by closely involving committed user communities;
  • Improving the quality of VRE user experience by providing user centred, secure, privacy compliant, sustainable environments for accessing data, composing workflows and tracking data publications;
  • Increasing VRE usage in multidisciplinary research domains by abstracting and reusing building blocks and workflows from existing VRE initiatives;
  • Improving the interoperability of heterogeneous discovery, contextual and detailed metadata across all layers of the VRE; and
  • Promoting the exploitation of VRE4EIC solutions to different research communities and commercially.
What personal data we collect and why we collect it
We collect only personal data from registered users, such as email, first and last name, company name, and company URL. This data is collected in order to create provenance information for metadata imported by users and is is not shared with anyone.
Who we share your data with
We don't share this information with anyone.
How long we retain your data
The personal information provided through the registration form is stored in a database. All users can see, edit, or delete their personal information at any time (except they cannot change their username).
